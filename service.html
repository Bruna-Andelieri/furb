Swimming Pool

Swimming is a great opportunity to have fun especially on hot days, and also an amazing source of exercise, improving muscular strength, reducing pain, releasing stress...

For those who need extra care, in our daycare, we provide a swimming pool for physiotherapy and surgery recovery.

Dog Training

Training is an essential part of owning a dog and can be started at any age. Training builds confidence, provides mental stimulation and strengthens the human-animal bond. Dogs are always learning. It is never too late to start training.

Dog Park

We have plenty of safe places for your pet, and it is super important for them to play with other dogs. Dogs are social animals, and playtime and positive interactions can facilitate brain development, social connections, and behavioral expectations.
